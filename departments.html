City Government Departments

Office of the Mayor

The executive department responsible for implementing city ordinances and administering the operations of the city government.

Contact: (046) 509-5289

Office of the Vice Mayor

Presides over the Sangguniang Panlungsod (City Council) and assumes the office of the mayor when necessary.

Contact: (046) 509-5290

Sangguniang Panlungsod

The legislative body that enacts ordinances, approves resolutions, and appropriates funds for the general welfare of the city.

Contact: (046) 509-5291

City Planning and Development Office

Formulates integrated economic, social, physical, and other development plans and policies for consideration of the city government.

Contact: (046) 509-5292

City Engineering Office

Responsible for infrastructure projects, public buildings, and other engineering-related concerns of the city.

Contact: (046) 509-5293

City Health Office

Provides health services, implements health programs, and monitors health-related concerns in the city.

Contact: (046) 509-5294

City Social Welfare and Development Office

Implements programs for the welfare and development of marginalized sectors of the community.

Contact: (046) 509-5295

City Treasurer's Office

Responsible for the custody of city funds, collection of taxes, fees, and other revenues.

Contact: (046) 509-5296
