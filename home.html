About Our City

General Trias, officially the City of General Trias, is a 1st class component city in the province of Cavite, Philippines. According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 450,583 people.

The city is named after General Mariano Trias, a revolutionary leader during the Philippine Revolution against Spain. It was formerly known as San Francisco de Malabon and was renamed in 1920 in honor of the general.

Today, General Trias is known for its rapid urbanization, excellent educational institutions, and growing industrial sector. The city government is committed to sustainable development and improving the quality of life for all its residents.

The Municipality of General Trias is a first-class component city in the province of Cavite, Philippines.
